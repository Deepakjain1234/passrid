PASSIT Vision

All great futuristic ideas somehow derive inspiration from what-is or what-was. So let us take an analogy from our Indian households. When we were kids, there were several articles in our homes that had combination locks and twister locks on them. Be it those bulky Aristocrat suitcases or those large Bajaj almirahs, our mothers used to write the combinations for all the various locks in a single diary and put that diary away in a locker, the key to which she kept close at all times. The combinations were revised and reset every quarter year, even if there wasnt much for anyone to take away. (Yeah GenZ! Thats how our homes functioned back in those days, maybe it still does.)

In comes, the Information age and the perils of an Indian house-maker become the nightmare of every single person out there. Thousands of websites are created every day and almost every one of them asks their visitors to create a digital ID to help them track your activity or remember for later or know us better. No matter the side benefits, one major mutual motive among all these authentications was collecting data. Why is it so important to collect this data? What good can knowing a persons email do to a multi-million-dollar business? These are some questions well discuss in a minute. According to several studies, a working adult, on average, visits 500+ different websites in a year, out of which around 40% provide access only after registration with a unique ID. That amounts to around 200 different Usernames and Passwords created in just a year. No dairy and locker is going to be sufficient enough to handle this amount of data.

Now back to our original analogy. Mind the Diary, the Locker, and its Key. Pass-It is all three rolled into one for all of your digital identities. We will store your passwords in an encrypted format in a single secure database personalized for your needs and provide you with a single master key to access all your digital identities.

Well explain the technicalities in a moment.

Now to our questions.

Why is it so important for almost every business out there to collect this data?

To answer this question, a concept called Subconscious Programming needs to be understood. We, as human beings, have an extraordinary ability to process data signals through various sensory input sources and store the chemical reactions that these signals produce and their effects in the form of subconscious memory. What we see throughout the day, what we smelled through a window we passed by, what feelings did that song induce, how ecstatic was that dish, all of these are signals that produce a subconscious memory. Subconscious memory in turn affects our decision-making to an extent no one thought possible. If you see a famous pop star wearing a designer jacket, you will want to get it. Even if you cannot afford the same jacket, youll probably settle for the same color or a look-alike. You see a famous food blogger's post appreciating burgers and immediately your mind wants a burger, you suppress the thought at that moment, but sometime next week youre walking acros a McDonald's outlet and the next thing you know is you're walking in and placing an order for a Big Mac.

Data and how it affects every single one of us is the true treasure of the information age. Now suppose you create 100 different Usernames and Passwords on 100 different sites but use your email id to do so. Now I can bet a million dollars (you'll never get it because even if I had such money, Ill win), there is a Big Brother sitting in his air-conditioned office with practically the map to your mind on his own personal computer. Yes, I'm talking about all the websites and web pages you visited. It shows what youre interested in. Do you like sneakers more or boots? Do you prefer rock music or classical melodies? What time of the day did you visit a food-delivery site? What is the time you're most active on the internet? Your digital signature holds all these answers and it is no longer between you and God. This data can be used to determine what advertisements you will see, what type of music will be at the top of your screen, what notifications from which websites you receive and at what time of the day will you receive them, and so on and so forth. Everything can be determined and somebody else now has the power to program your subconscious to suit the profit clause and cause of his company and business.

That is why data collection and manipulation are so important to these businesses. It will not be an understatement to say that the very survival of these businesses depends on this data that we so willingly provide.

Steve Jobs once said Everything around you that you call life was made up by people that were no smarter than you. And you can change it, you can influence it Once you learn that, you'll never be the same again

What is to stop Big Brother from underhanding that data to a rival business for a reasonable price under the cover of a sealed settlement deal? NOTHING. NOT A THING. We so often hear about data leaks but we never hear about data deals.

Now it can also be asked 'What is to stop us from taking that power from Big Brother? again the answer is NOTHING. But we need the right tool to do that. And Pass-It is an attempt to develop such a tool. It is the first step towards taking that power to program our minds back in our own hands, towards protecting our privacy, and towards easing the burden of memorizing the keys to our own digital identities.

The future will be ruled by Data and Information. But it is in our hands whether we follow this path to a Monarchy or we reinstate the road to Democracy.

So to answer the previous question.

Our Vision is to create a platform that people believe empowers them to take the control of their ever-expanding digital lives in their own hands and enables them to live as they wish without a threat to their privacy. Our Mission is to create this platform in a certain way so as to meet the need of every person who wishes to avail of its services and do it in the most amiable way possible. Our (current) Goal is to build this platform and a satisfied and empowered community of 100,000 users in the next three years.
